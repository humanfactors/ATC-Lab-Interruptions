Interruptions

What are Interruptions?

Sometimes during a scenario you may have to manage an additional ATC scenario. When this happens, you will see a white + symbol on a black screen for 3 seconds to warn you of the impending interruption. Then a different (but similar) sector will appear for you to manage for 27 seconds. You will not be able to see or interact with the primary scenario for the duration of the interruption.

Within a disrupting scenario, you still need to accept and handoff aircraft, and detect and resolve any conflicts just as you would at any other point in time.

Acceptances and handoffs in the primary scenario will be temporarily managed for you whilst you are taken away from the sector. However, conflicts will not be automatically resolved.

Your performance on these trials is factored into your final score.
