Recap and Points

Recap

  • Aircraft enter and exit your sector
  • You accept orange aircraft entering by pressing the key.
  • You handoff blue aircraft existing by pressing the key.
  • Conflicts occur if aircraft will come within 1000ft of each other vertically, and 5nm horizontally (yellow marker). You need to prevent conflicts leading to violations by clicking the altitude indicator and changing the altitude to a different number.

Points

You are awarded and subtracted points based on your performance. The experimenter can provide you feedback on your score at the end of the experiment.

  Correctly performing handoff or accept  +10 Points
  Detecing a Conflict (reward increases with speed)  +10 - 40 Points 
  Missing a handoff or accept  - 10 Points 
  Letting a conflict turn into violation (let it turn yellow)   - 40 Points 
  Changing altitude if not in conflict or after it has already turned yellow.  - 40 Points
