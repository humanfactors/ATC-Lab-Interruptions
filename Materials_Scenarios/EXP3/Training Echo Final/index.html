En Route Air Traffic Control
Simulation Experiment

Welcome to the Air Traffic Control Simulator Experiment. In this experiment you will complete a task that is similar to the popular mobile game, “Flight Control”.

The entire session will last approximately 120 minutes. Please put your phone on silent and place it in your bag before continuing.

This training program will teach you how to keep an air traffic control sector operating safely. By the end of the training you will understand:

  • What a flight control sector is.
  • How to read an aircraft information box.
  • How to accept and handoff aircraft (and what these tasks are).
  • How to identify and resolve conflicts (and what this task is).
  • How to manage potential disruptions.

Read the information in this training carefully, however it should not take you longer than 15 minutes to get to the end. If you are stuck on a concept, please do not hesitate to contact the experimenter.
